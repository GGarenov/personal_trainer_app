You've heard you need protein within 30 minutes of training. The truth is more forgiving: total daily protein drives most of your results.

Aim for 1.6–2.2 g per kg of body weight spread across 3–5 meals. If you hit that target consistently, obsessing over the post-workout shake timing won't make or break your progress.
