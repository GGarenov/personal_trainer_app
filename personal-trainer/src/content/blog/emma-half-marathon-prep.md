Emma wanted to run her first half marathon but also drop body fat before race day. Most coaches would say pick one — we structured both.

Two strength sessions per week preserved muscle while she built running volume gradually. Nutrition stayed at a mild deficit with carbs timed around long runs. Race day she crossed the line in 1:52 and was down 8 kg from her starting weight.

Hybrid goals are possible when the plan is periodized instead of thrown together week to week.
