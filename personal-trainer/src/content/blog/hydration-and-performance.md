Most people walk into the gym already dehydrated and don't realize it. Even a 2% drop in body water can reduce strength output and increase perceived effort.

A simple baseline: drink half your body weight in pounds as ounces of water daily, then add 500 ml for every hour of training. In hot weather or long sessions, add electrolytes — sodium and potassium matter when you sweat heavily.

Check your urine color. Pale yellow is the target. Dark and concentrated means you need more fluids before you chase another rep PR.
