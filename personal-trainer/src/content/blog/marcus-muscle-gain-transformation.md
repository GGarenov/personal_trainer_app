Marcus came to me at 68 kg and 185 cm — classic hardgainer. He'd tried every mass gainer on the market and still couldn't fill out a t-shirt.

We ran a controlled bulk: 300 kcal surplus, four lifting days per week, and a step target to keep fat gain in check. No dirty bulking, no skipping leg day. Twenty weeks later he was 80 kg on the scale with visible abs and a 100 kg bench press.

The lesson isn't that bulking is magic — it's that consistent surplus plus progressive overload beats random eating every time.
