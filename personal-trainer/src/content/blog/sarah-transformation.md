Sarah came to me frustrated after years of yo-yo dieting. We focused on a moderate calorie deficit, three strength sessions per week, and daily steps.

Twelve weeks later, she was down 18 kg, her squat had increased by 40 kg, and — most importantly — she had a plan she could maintain long after the program ended.
