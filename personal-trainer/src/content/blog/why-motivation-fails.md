Everyone feels motivated on January 1st. By February, most people have quit. The difference between those who transform and those who don't isn't willpower — it's structure.

Build non-negotiable habits, remove friction from good choices, and add friction to bad ones. Pair up with someone who expects you at the gym. Motivation gets you started; systems keep you going.
