You don't need two hours in the gym six days a week to transform your body. You need a plan that respects your calendar and still drives progress.

Full-body workouts three times per week are the sweet spot for busy professionals. Hit squat or deadlift, a push, and a pull each session. Superset accessories to save time. Cap sessions at 45–60 minutes — intensity beats duration when time is limited.

Protect your training slots like meetings. Non-negotiable calendar blocks beat motivation every time when life gets chaotic.
