The squat, bench press, and deadlift are the foundation of serious strength training. But doing them and doing them well are two very different things.

For the squat, think "spread the floor" with your feet and keep your chest up through the sticking point. On bench, retract your shoulder blades and drive your feet into the ground — leg drive is free stability. For deadlifts, pull the slack out of the bar before you move and keep the bar close to your shins the entire way up.

Film your top sets once a month. What feels right often looks very different on camera, and small technical fixes compound into big PRs over time.
