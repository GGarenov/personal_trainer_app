Progressive overload is the foundation of every effective training program. But most lifters think it only means adding more plates to the bar.

In reality, you can progress through increased reps, improved technique, shorter rest periods, or better mind-muscle connection. The key is tracking your workouts and ensuring you're doing more work over time — however that looks for your current level.
