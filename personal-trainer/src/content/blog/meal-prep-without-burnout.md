You don't need to cook seven different meals every Sunday. The most sustainable meal prep systems are built on repetition, not variety.

Start with two protein sources, two carb bases, and a rotation of vegetables. Batch cook once, portion into containers, and mix throughout the week. Consistency beats perfection every time.
